days of mr Julius Nyerere

  • 1934: Nyerere began his education at the Native Administration School in Mwisenge
  • 1941: Nyerere completed his secondary education and decided to study at Makerere College in the Ugandan city of Kampala
  • 1947: He spend his time reading and farming in butiama
  • 1961: He governed Tanganyika as prime minister
  • 1962: He governed tanganiyika as president
  • 1999: Died in london united kingdom
